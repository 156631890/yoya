Company Profile

A Yiwu factory business built around practical product development and long-term B2B supply.

Yiwu Nanjian Hosiery Co., Ltd. focuses on activewear, seamless apparel, hosiery and related private label cooperation. The business combines in-house manufacturing conditions with export-oriented communication so buyers can move from product selection to inquiry more efficiently.

Who We Are

Factory-first, buyer-facing

Our website and product structure are written for importers, brand owners and sourcing teams that need fast access to product categories, capability signals and inquiry routes. The emphasis is on useful commercial information rather than broad corporate slogans.

What We Make

Activewear, seamless apparel and hosiery

The current range highlights sports bras, jumpsuits, yoga tops, leggings, shorts, sets and selected menswear. The product mix reflects practical export demand and supports both stock-style selection and custom development conversations.

How We Work

Clear communication from sample to order

We support inquiry review, sampling discussion, specification alignment and production follow-up in a structured way so buyers can understand scope, category direction and next steps early in the process.

Brand Background

Nanjian and Limeiqun form the commercial identity behind the factory business.

Nanjian is used as the factory-facing business identity on this independent website. Limeiqun remains an established product and market reference tied to the company's long-running manufacturing background in seamless and knitted categories.

  • Nanjian is positioned for direct B2B communication and inquiry handling.
  • Limeiqun reflects the company's historical market presence and product familiarity.
  • Both names connect back to Yiwu Nanjian Hosiery Co., Ltd. and its manufacturing base.
Our Approach
