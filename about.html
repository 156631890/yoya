About Us

Yiwu Nanjian Hosiery Co., Ltd. and the Limeiqun brand.

Nanjian Hosiery Co., Ltd. is located in Yiwu City, Yinan Industrial Zone. Benefiting from the local business environment, the company has expanded attention in domestic and overseas markets through diversified hosiery and underwear manufacturing.

Company facts

  • Registered capital: RMB 10 million.
  • Total assets: RMB 320 million.
  • Factory area: over 10,000 square meters.
  • Building area: more than 13,000 square meters.

Main products

Socks, underwear, slimming tights, silk stockings, pantyhose, seamless shapewear, natural colored cotton underwear, bras and more.

Brand history

Nanjian officially settled on Alibaba on April 1, 2012. Limeiqun was officially established on December 9, 2016 as the flagship brand under Nanjian Hosiery.

Brand positioning

Limeiqun targets urban fashion consumers aged 18 to 65 with diverse styles, rapid updates and cost-effective products influenced by Korean fashion trends.
