Process

A homepage explanation of how buyers move from inquiry to production.

1

Share your category brief

Send product type, estimated quantity, target market and whether you need stock support, OEM or private label development.

2

Review references and sampling

We align on suitable styles, construction direction and next-step sample or product-reference discussion.

3

Confirm production details

Order quantities, materials, packaging direction and delivery requirements are clarified before bulk execution.

4

Move into follow-through

After confirmation, the project continues through production coordination and communication based on the agreed scope.
